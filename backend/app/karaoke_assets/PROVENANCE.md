# Bundled karaoke fonts

All files are redistributed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1. Fontsource assets are copied from the exact package tarballs recorded in `web/package-lock.json`; no font is fetched at runtime. The renderer uses the selected display face for Latin text and switches to bundled Noto fallback faces for non-Latin/symbol ranges. Coverage is limited to glyphs present in these files; unsupported glyphs may remain missing.
